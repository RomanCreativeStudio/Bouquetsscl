The Person Behind Every Bouquet

I Started This Because I Wanted to Make People Smile.

I am Carina. I have been making bouquets for three years out of my home in Avon Park, FL. I did not go to school for this. I just loved making them and I loved even more the way someone's face lights up when they get one.

That is the whole thing, honestly. I love seeing someone open a bouquet and just stop for a second because they were not expecting it to look that good. That reaction is what I am going for every time I sit down to make an order.

I work in foam, ribbon, crochet, pipe cleaner, natural flowers, and artificial flowers. Every order is custom. You can bring me inspiration, a color palette, a vague idea, or nothing at all. I will work with whatever you give me.

FAQ

Things People Ask Before They Order.

  • A 50% deposit is required for customizable arrangements. It helps me cover the cost of materials I need to purchase specifically for your order. You can reach me through the contact form or the "customize your bouquet" option. Feel free to send inspiration photos, color ideas, or just tell me what you are going for.

  • Pickup is at 1310 W Allamanda Blvd, Avon Park, FL. We will coordinate a time when you confirm your order. If you prefer delivery, I offer local delivery for an extra fee, and I will tell you the cost upfront before anything is finalized.
